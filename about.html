About Our Pizzeria

Welcome to Tommy's

Tommy's Pizza is a beloved family-owned pizzeria that has proudly served the South Richmond Hill and Ozone Park communities for nearly six decades. Since opening our doors in 1967, we've been dedicated to crafting the finest authentic New York-style pizza — made fresh daily with premium ingredients and recipes passed down through generations.

Located in the heart of Liberty Avenue, we've become a neighborhood institution: a place where families gather, friends meet, and everyone feels at home. Whether you're grabbing a quick slice on your lunch break or ordering pies for a family celebration, Tommy's delivers the taste of authentic Queens Italian food that keeps people coming back.

From our famous crispy crusts to our perfectly seasoned sauce and generous layers of mozzarella, every pizza we make is a testament to quality and tradition. We're not just making pizza — we're making memories for families throughout Queens.

How It Started

One Shop, Big Dreams

When Tommy's Pizza first opened on Liberty Avenue in 1967, the neighborhood quickly embraced this new family-owned pizzeria. What started as a small shop with big dreams has grown into one of the most trusted names for pizza in South Richmond Hill, Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, and the surrounding Queens neighborhoods.

For nearly six decades, we've watched children grow up on our pizza, become parents themselves, and bring their own kids in for the same slices they loved. That's the legacy we're proudest of — being part of your family's story, one pizza at a time.

Our recipes haven't changed because they don't need to. The same time-honored techniques and quality ingredients that made us a neighborhood favorite are still in the kitchen today.
