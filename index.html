Our Story

A Queens Tradition Since 1967

Tommy's opened on Liberty Avenue in 1967 and never left. For nearly six decades we've watched kids grow up on our slices, become parents, and bring their own children in for the same pies.

The recipes haven't changed because they don't need to — dough made fresh every morning, our own sauce, premium mozzarella, baked to order.
